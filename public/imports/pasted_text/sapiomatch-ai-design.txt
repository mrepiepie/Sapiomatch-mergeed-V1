Create a complete futuristic AI education-matching website design in Figma for a platform called “SapioMatch AI”.

The platform is an AI-powered education discovery and matching marketplace. It helps students, working professionals, and future learners find the best universities, academies, training providers, and professional programs based on their goals, academic background, budget, preferred location, delivery mode, and career direction.

Design style:
Create a premium, futuristic, deep AI technology visual identity. The design should feel modern, intelligent, trustworthy, and exciting — not like a basic education website. Use a dark futuristic theme with deep navy, black, midnight blue, purple, cyan, and electric blue accents. Use glowing gradients, glassmorphism cards, soft neon borders, floating UI panels, subtle grid backgrounds, AI particles, abstract light trails, and futuristic dashboard previews. It should look like a high-end AI SaaS product combined with an education/career platform.

Do not make it childish or too “school website”. It should feel like an advanced AI platform for serious learners, working professionals, universities, and academies.

Visual inspiration:
Combine the best concepts from:

1. Career mapping platforms: personalized path visualization, match scores, future path cards, skill gaps, and AI recommendations.
2. Career discovery platforms: simple guided journey, clean “explore your possibilities” messaging, human-centered motivation, and step-by-step onboarding.
3. AI education tools: guided input forms, no-prompting-required workflows, clean cards, generated recommendations, and structured educational outputs.
4. AI school/platform websites: bold hero section, trust/safety emphasis, stats, testimonials, purple AI branding, glowing cards, and strong CTA buttons.

1. Homepage

Primary CTA:
“Find My Best Program”

“Explore Institutions”

Hero visual:
On the right side, create a futuristic AI matching dashboard mockup. It should include:

* A glowing AI orb or digital brain
* A “92% Match Score” card
* Floating recommended program cards
* Program examples such as “Global MBA”, “AI Business Diploma”, “Cybersecurity Academy”
* Mini cards showing: Budget Fit, Location Fit, Career Alignment, Delivery Mode
* A glowing “AI Recommendation Engine” label

Homepage sections:

1. “How It Works”
   Show a clean 4-step process:

* Enter your goals
* AI analyzes your profile
* View ranked programs
* Connect with institutions

2. “Why Choose SapioMatch AI”
   Use 4 futuristic cards:

* AI Matching
* Verified Institution Data
* Smart Program Comparison
* Career-Focused Recommendations

3. “Explore Your Possibilities”
   Inspired by career-path platforms. Show a visual career/education map with dotted lines from the user profile to multiple program cards. Include matching percentages and small labels like “Best Fit”, “Flexible Schedule”, “Career Boost”, and “Budget Friendly”.

4. “Trusted Data, Better Decisions”
   Explain that institutions submit and update their own information, while admins validate before publication. This is important: the institution is responsible for maintaining its own academic and commercial data, not the admin.

   Right: For universities, academies, and training providers.

   Button: “Start Matching Now”

About Us page requirements:
The About Us page should explain the platform’s purpose in a futuristic but trustworthy way.

Main headline:
“A smarter bridge between learners and trusted education providers.”

Content:
Explain that SapioMatch AI helps users make confident education decisions by matching them with suitable programs using AI, verified institution data, and transparent comparison logic.

Include sections:

1. “The Problem We Solve”
   Talk about scattered information, confusing program options, unclear requirements, and difficulty comparing institutions.

2. “Why Matching Matters”
   Explain that the best program is not always the most famous one; it is the one that fits the learner’s background, budget, schedule, location, goals, and career direction.

3. “How We Help Users”
   Cards:

* Personalized recommendations
* Program comparison
* Match scores
* Saved results
* Guided decision-making

4. “How We Help Institutions”
   Cards:

* Institution-controlled profiles
* Program visibility
* Qualified leads
* Admin-validated publication
* Commercial and lead access rules

5. “Our Operating Model”
   Show this as a modern matrix or process:
   Institution submits/updates data → Admin validates → Approved data is published → Users see matching results → Leads become visible based on permissions and credit rules → Super Admin controls governance and commercial rules.
